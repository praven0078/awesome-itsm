# Awesome ITSM [![Awesome](https://awesome.re/badge.svg)](https://awesome.re)

> A curated list of outstanding resources for IT Service Management professionals — frameworks, tools, certifications, communities, AI tooling, metrics, and templates.

IT Service Management (ITSM) is the discipline of designing, delivering, managing, and improving the way IT is used within an organisation. This list is maintained by a practitioner with 27 years of ITSM experience across India, New Zealand, and Australia.

## Frameworks and Standards

- [ITIL 4](https://www.axelos.com/certifications/itil-service-management) - The most widely adopted ITSM framework globally. Covers the service value system, four dimensions model, and 34 management practices.
- [COBIT 2019](https://www.isaca.org/resources/cobit) - Governance and management framework for enterprise IT, published by ISACA.
- [ISO/IEC 20000](https://www.iso.org/standard/70636.html) - International standard for IT service management. The formal certification path for ITSM at the organisational level.
- [VeriSM](https://verism.global/) - Modern service management model designed for the digital age, wrapping existing frameworks under a governance umbrella.
- [SIAM](https://www.siamfoundation.com/) - Service Integration and Management — the framework for managing multiple service providers in a complex IT supply chain.

## Certifications

- [ITIL 4 Foundation](https://www.axelos.com/certifications/itil-service-management/itil-4-foundation) - Entry-level certification covering core ITSM concepts. Industry baseline for practitioners.
- [ITIL 4 Managing Professional (MP)](https://www.axelos.com/certifications/itil-service-management/managing-professional) - Advanced track covering practical and technical skills for running IT-enabled services.
- [ITIL 4 Strategic Leader (SL)](https://www.axelos.com/certifications/itil-service-management/strategic-leader) - For professionals who influence business strategy using IT.
- [COBIT 5 Foundation](https://www.isaca.org/credentialing/cobit) - Governance framework certification by ISACA.
- [ServiceNow CSA](https://www.servicenow.com/services/training-and-certification/certified-system-administrator.html) - Certified System Administrator. Core ServiceNow platform certification.
- [ServiceNow CIS-ITSM](https://www.servicenow.com/services/training-and-certification/certified-implementation-specialist-itsm.html) - Certified Implementation Specialist for ITSM. Validates ability to implement ITSM on the Now Platform.
- [HDI Certifications](https://www.thinkhdi.com/education/certifications) - Support centre-focused certifications including HDI-SCA, HDI-SCM, and HDI-DST.
- [SDI Certifications](https://www.sdi.co.uk/qualifications) - Service Desk Institute certifications for service desk professionals.

## AI Tools for ITSM

- [Claude](https://claude.ai/) - AI assistant well-suited to ITSM documentation, root cause analysis drafts, process design, and knowledge article generation.
- [ServiceNow Now Assist](https://www.servicenow.com/now-platform/now-assist.html) - Generative AI capabilities embedded natively in ServiceNow across ITSM, ITOM, and CSM.
- [Atlassian Intelligence](https://www.atlassian.com/software/artificial-intelligence) - AI features across Jira Service Management for summarisation, auto-classification, and virtual agents.
- [Make.com](https://www.make.com/) - No-code automation platform for connecting ITSM tools, ticketing systems, and notification workflows. *(Affiliate)*
- [Notion AI](https://www.notion.so/product/ai) - AI-powered workspace useful for ITSM knowledge bases, runbooks, and SOP management. *(Affiliate)*
- [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io/) - AI voice synthesis — useful for creating ITSM training content and process walkthrough videos. *(Affiliate)*
- [CodePlain](https://pravenite.gumroad.com/l/oflsgt) - PowerShell-based tool that explains scripts and automation code in plain English. Useful for ITSM admins managing ServiceNow scripting without a development background.

## Metrics and KPIs

- [ITIL KPI Library — Axelos](https://www.axelos.com/resource-hub/white-paper/itil-4-key-metrics) - Official Axelos guidance on measuring ITSM practices across the service value chain.
- [HDI Support Center Metrics](https://www.thinkhdi.com/library/supportworld/2023/metrics-that-matter.aspx) - Industry benchmarks for service desk performance including FCR, AHT, CSAT, and abandonment rate.

## Books

- [ITIL 4 Foundation](https://www.axelos.com/store/book/itil-foundation-itil-4-edition) - The core ITIL 4 text published by Axelos. Essential reading for any ITSM practitioner.
- [The Phoenix Project](https://itrevolution.com/product/the-phoenix-project/) - Gene Kim, Kevin Behr, George Spafford. Novel-format book demonstrating ITSM, DevOps, and lean principles in practice. Widely recommended as an introduction to modern IT operations.
- [The DevOps Handbook](https://itrevolution.com/product/the-devops-handbook-second-edition/) - Gene Kim et al. Practical companion to The Phoenix Project. Bridges ITSM and DevOps practices.
- [The ServiceNow Developer's Handbook](https://www.amazon.com/ServiceNow-Developers-Handbook-Tim-Woodruff/dp/1801074860) - Tim Woodruff. Considered the definitive reference for ServiceNow administrators and developers.
- [Lean IT](https://itrevolution.com/product/lean-it/) - Steve Bell and Mike Orzen. Applies lean manufacturing principles to IT operations and service management.

## Tools and Platforms

- [ServiceNow](https://www.servicenow.com/) - Enterprise ITSM platform. Market leader for large and mid-enterprise ITSM, ITOM, and ITBM.
- [Jira Service Management](https://www.atlassian.com/software/jira/service-management) - Atlassian's ITSM platform. Strong in software-forward organisations using Confluence and Jira already.
- [Freshservice](https://freshservice.com/) - Cloud-based ITSM platform well suited to mid-market organisations. Strong AI features and competitive pricing.
- [Ivanti Neurons](https://www.ivanti.com/products/ivanti-neurons-for-itsm) - ITSM platform with strong asset management and endpoint management integration.
- [BMC Helix](https://www.bmc.com/it-solutions/bmc-helix-itsm.html) - Enterprise ITSM and AI operations platform from BMC. Competitor to ServiceNow in large enterprises.
- [Zendesk](https://www.zendesk.com/) - Customer service and ITSM platform. Strong for organisations with a significant external customer service function alongside internal IT.
- [xMatters](https://www.xmatters.com/) - Incident management and on-call scheduling. Integrates with ServiceNow, Jira, PagerDuty.
- [PagerDuty](https://www.pagerduty.com/) - Digital operations management platform for major incident response and on-call management.
